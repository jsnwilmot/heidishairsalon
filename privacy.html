Website privacy

Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Heidi's Hair Salon handles information connected to this website and appointment inquiries.

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Information You Provide

If you contact Heidi's Hair Salon by phone, email, Facebook, or another booking method, you may choose to provide your name, phone number, email address, appointment preferences, and details about the hair service you are requesting. This information is used to respond to inquiries, discuss services, provide quotes, and manage appointments.

Website Analytics

This website uses Google Analytics to understand general website traffic, such as pages visited, device or browser type, approximate location, and how visitors interact with the site. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to collect and process this information.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored by your browser. This site may use cookies through Google Analytics to help measure website performance and visitor activity. You can adjust cookie settings in your browser or use browser tools to block or delete cookies.

Third-Party Links

This website links to third-party services, including Google reviews and Facebook. Those websites have their own privacy practices, and their policies apply when you visit them.

Information Sharing

Heidi's Hair Salon does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to provide services, respond to your request, use website tools such as Google Analytics, or comply with legal obligations.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Heidi's Hair Salon at HeidisHairSalon79@gmail.com or call 604-345-5097.
