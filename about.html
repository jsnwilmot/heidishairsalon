One-on-one hair care

A Smaller Salon Visit with Time to Talk

Heidi's Hair Salon is a home-based salon in Lethbridge built around one-on-one service. The appointment feels quieter and more personal than a large salon floor, which can be helpful when you want to explain your hair history, ask questions, and understand your options.

Heidi works with clients on haircuts, colour, highlights, styling, treatments, smoothing, and seniors' hair services. Appointments are required, so please call or email before visiting.
