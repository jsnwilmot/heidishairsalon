One-on-one hair care

Hi, I'm Heidi.

I provide one-on-one hair services from a private home salon in Lethbridge. My goal is to make every appointment feel relaxed, personal, and focused on what works best for your hair.

Clients visit for haircuts, colour, highlights, styling, treatments, seniors' hair services, men's cuts, kids' cuts, and family hair care. Appointments are never rushed, and you have time to talk through your hair history, quote details, and the result you want.
